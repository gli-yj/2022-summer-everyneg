Instructions

You will see short excerpts of conversations from American radio and TV programs that took place between 1990 and 2012. Each excerpt will contain a bolded sentence or sentence part.

Your task is to choose the best paraphrase for the bolded part. What do you think the speaker was trying to say? Please read the paraphrases carefully!

Most of the transcripts look like this:

@!Speaker A: speech speech speech ...

@!Speaker B: speech speech speech ...
